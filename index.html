Buffalo Chicken Ramen

A quick an easy ramen recipe that is not only inexpensive, but tastes amazing!

INGREDIENTS & NECESSITIES

  • 1 (one) pack of Top Ramen Noodles (Any Flavor)
  • Boneless Buffalo Chicken Bites
  • Siracha Sauce
  • Air Fryer or Conventional Oven
  • Stovetop
  • Small pot
  • Pasta Strainer
  • Water

COOKING STEPS

  1. Begin by preheating your Air Fryer or Conventional oven to 365 degrees Farenheit.
  2. Add water to the small pot and begin boiling it on the stovetop.
  3. Once Air Fryer or oven is heated, add 5 (five) Buffalo Chicken Bites. Set cook to 11 minutes
  4. Add Top Ramen noodles to boiling water once ready
  5. Cook noodles for 3 minutes and strain once done
  6. Chop up cooked buffalo chicken bites and add to ramen noodles
  7. Add siracha to chicken and ramen
  8. ENJOY!
