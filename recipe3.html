Avocado Toast

An easy breakfast option for college students who have a late start to class!

INGREDIENTS & NECESSITIES

  • Wholly Guacamole Avocado Paste
  • Any type of bread
  • Salt
  • Crushed Red Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  • Utensils (Spoon and Knife)
  • A Toaster or Toaster Oven

COOKING STEPS

  1. Begin by toasting your bread.
  2. Once toast is perfect, take your knife and spread your Wholly Guacamole Avocado paste on your toast.
  3. Sprinkle your crushed red pepper and salt on top of your toast
  4. Pour some olive oil into a spoon and drizzle it on your slice of toast
  5. Repeat this for your second slice of tost
  6. Put slices of toast on your serving plate.
  7. ENJOY!
