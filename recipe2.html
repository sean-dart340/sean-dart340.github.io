Cesar Salad

A healthy option for when you need a quick meal in between classes!

INGREDIENTS & NECESSITIES

  • Romainne Lettuce
  • Shredded Parmesean Cheese
  • Croutons
  • Cesar Dressing
  • Mixing Bowl

COOKING STEPS

  1. Start by washing your Romainee Lettuce with water and pat dry with a paper towel.
  2. Chop Lettuce into chunks until they are a size comfortable enough to eat whole.
  3. Add your lettuce and croutons into the mixing bowl.
  4. Drizzle Cesar dressing onto the croutons and lettuce
  5. Mix thoroughly
  6. Sprinkle the shredded parmesean cheese into mixing bowl
  7. Put your salad in to a serving bowl.
  8. ENJOY!
